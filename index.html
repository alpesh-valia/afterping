About AfterPing

AfterPing is a powerful Android application designed to help you manage and organize your notification history. Never lose important notifications again! AfterPing captures, stores, and organizes all your notifications locally on your device, giving you complete control over your notification data.

With AfterPing, you can search through past notifications, create reminders, organize by apps, and gain insights into your notification patterns‚Äîall while keeping your data private and secure on your device.

Privacy & Security

AfterPing is designed with your privacy as the top priority:

  • Local Storage Only: All your data is stored exclusively on your device
  • No Data Transmission: We don't send any data to remote servers
  • No Personal Information: We don't collect names, emails, or any PII
  • No Tracking: We don't track your usage patterns or behavior
  • Full Control: You can delete all data at any time
  • Open Source: The code is open source and auditable
