# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** December 2024

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, stored, and protected when you use the AfterPing mobile application (the "App").

## Data Collection and Storage

### What Is Collected

AfterPing collects and stores the following information **on your phone only**:

- **Notification Data**: 
  - Notification titles, text content, and metadata
  - App package names and notification icons
  - Timestamps of notifications
  - Notification channel information
  
- **App Information**:
  - Installed app names and package identifiers
  - App icons and metadata
  - Notification statistics per app

- **User Preferences**:
  - Favorite notifications
  - Archived notifications
  - Reminder settings
  - App organization preferences (tags, pinning, etc.)

### What Is NOT Collected

- **Personal Information**: AfterPing does not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personally identifiable information (PII)
- **Location Data**: AfterPing does not collect GPS coordinates or location information
- **Device Identifiers**: AfterPing does not collect device IDs, IMEI, or advertising IDs
- **Network Data**: AfterPing does not collect IP addresses, MAC addresses, or network information
- **Usage Analytics**: AfterPing does not track your usage patterns or app interactions
- **Third-Party Data**: AfterPing does not collect data from third-party services or social media accounts

## Data Storage

### Local Storage Only

**All data collected by AfterPing is stored exclusively on your phone.** AfterPing does not:

- Transmit any data to remote servers
- Sync data to cloud services
- Share data with third parties
- Store data in external databases
- Use analytics or tracking services

### Data Storage on Your Device

- All notification history and app data is encrypted and stored in your phone's internal storage
- Data is encrypted to protect your privacy and secured using your device's built-in security features
- You have full control over your data and can delete it at any time through the App

## Data Usage

### How Your Data Is Used

The collected data is used **only** to provide the core functionality of the App:

- **Notification History**: Display and organize your notification history
- **Search Functionality**: Enable searching through past notifications
- **Reminders**: Create and manage reminders based on notifications
- **Organization**: Allow you to organize notifications by apps, tags, favorites, etc.
- **Statistics**: Provide insights about your notification patterns

### Permissions Required

AfterPing requires the following permissions to function:

- **Notification Access**: To capture and store notifications
- **Post Notifications**: To display reminder notifications (Android 13+)
- **Exact Alarm**: To schedule precise reminder alarms (Android 12+)
- **Battery Optimization Exemption**: To ensure reminders work reliably

All permissions are used **solely** for the App's core functionality and are not used for any other purpose.

## Data Security

### Security Measures

- **Phone Storage Only**: All data is stored on your phone, reducing the risk of external breaches
- **No Internet Transmission**: Since we don't send data over the internet, there's no risk of interception during transmission
- **Device Security**: We rely on your device's built-in security features for data protection
- **No Remote Servers**: We have no servers or cloud storage that could be compromised

### Your Responsibility

- You are responsible for maintaining the security of your phone
- It is recommended to enable phone encryption and screen locks
- Uninstalling the App will delete all stored data

## Data Deletion

### Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

- **Delete Individual Items**: Delete specific notifications or apps at any time
- **Bulk Deletion**: Delete multiple items using selection mode
- **Archive**: Archive notifications to remove them from the main view
- **Uninstall**: Uninstalling the App permanently deletes all stored data

### How to Delete Data

1. Open the App
2. Select the items you want to delete
3. Use the delete action, or
4. Uninstall the App to delete all data

## Third-Party Services

### AdMob

AfterPing uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

- **Device Information**: Device type, OS version, language
- **Advertising ID**: Android Advertising ID (can be reset in device settings)
- **Location**: General location based on IP address (approximate)

**We do not share your notification data with AdMob.** AdMob's data collection is governed by [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

You can opt-out of personalized ads in your device settings:
- **Android**: Settings → Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID / Opt out of Ads Personalization

## Children's Privacy

AfterPing is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
